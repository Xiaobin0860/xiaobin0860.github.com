Lists
-----

Lists can contain anything! `[Element1, Element2, ..., ElementN]`

    1> [1, 2, 3, {numbers,[4,5,6]}, 5.34, atom].
    [1,2,3,{numbers,[4,5,6]},5.34,atom]

    2> [97, 98, 99].
    "abc"

    [New] ++ [OldList]

BIFS

    hd([1,2,3,4]).
    tl([1,2,3,4]).
    length(List).

pattern matching:

    [Head|Tail].
